Our Story

Shyamal Patel had been working in an independent pharmacy after graduating from MCPHS University, Boston – MA in 2017. It all began in 2019, when he was introduced to Hemp / CBD products by a fellow pharmacist. Shyamal started selling CBD products in the pharmacy and in no time, he was able to get around 450 patients to buy CBD products on regular basis. Committed to helping local community, he introduced CBD products to his younger brother, Lay Patel, who owns a local convenience store in Chester – VA.

Lay started selling the CBD products that were recommended by his brother. Lay with no medicinal background, would have hard time initially to explain all the details of the product and its uses. He would Facetime Shyamal at the Pharmacy, and counselling was done for customers in the C-store regarding the dose and product recommendations at no cost.

•The issue that both brothers were facing was the pricing. CBD products were not cheap to buy and then you would have to sell it at a price which is profitable in retail setting. One thing that both brothers noted was that people those who were struggling financially were not able to purchase the products. Brothers started to break the stock bottles and gave away unit dose gummies at $3 per gummy cost. It lasted for few months, but again they were not able to help all the clienteles because not everyone had that $60-$100 to spend on monthly basis. Brothers decided to open their own CBD company that can substantially reduce the price of the products and provide better quality as well. Both brothers would like to thank Uresha Patel, who is happily married to Shyamal Patel, for providing them with an incredible company name – Moody Moon. With the background in pharmacy, Shyamal started to formulate CBD gummies for local customers while Lay did all the market research for CBD. With the great response from the local community, they have expanded their product line and are doing well today! They are in about 120 retail locations including Vape shops, CBD-Stores, C-Stores, Gas Stations, and Independent Community Pharmacies.
